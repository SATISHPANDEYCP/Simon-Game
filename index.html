Simon Says Game

Welcome to Simon Says!

Simon Says is an engaging memory game where you need to repeat a sequence of colors shown by the game. The sequence gets longer and more complex as you advance through levels.

Game Rules:

  • Objective: Memorize and repeat the sequence of colors shown by the game. Each round adds a new color to the sequence.
  • Starting the Game: The game begins when you click anywhere on your screen. The game will display a sequence of colors one by one.
  • Following the Sequence: After the sequence is shown, replicate it by clicking the colored buttons in the same order. Success will advance you to the next level.
  • Levels and Scoring: Each correct sequence increases your level and score. Your score is the product of your current level and score.
  • Incorrect Sequence: If you click the buttons incorrectly, the game ends. Your final score will be shown, and you can start over.
  • High Score: The game tracks your highest score. If you achieve a new high score, it will be displayed after the game ends.
  • Tips: Carefully remember the sequence and react quickly to score the highest possible. The game becomes more challenging as you progress.
